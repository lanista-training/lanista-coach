## Beschreibung

There are 3 different scenarios that influence the layout of this conponent:
<ul>
  <li>Exercise in a workout in a customer context</li>
  <li>Exercise to protocoll (in a customer context)</li>
  <li>Exercise without customer conttext</li>
  <li>Exercise in a workout without a customer context</li>
  <li>Exercise is in edit mode</li>
</ul>
To identify the context two parameters are necessary:
<ul>
  <li>customer_id</li>
  <li>workout_id</li>
  <li>editable</li>
</ul>
